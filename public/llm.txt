## About

Eric Caskey is an Enterprise Platform Engineer at Amazon, based in the NYC metro area. He builds microservice-based software platforms and change-safety systems that keep infrastructure current, reliable, and safe as adoption scales across teams.

He architected and drove adoption of a monitor deployment platform that integrates internal tools and AWS CloudWatch through microservices. The system continuously ingests infrastructure change signals from multiple APIs, S3 datasets, and SNS notifications to keep monitors up to date. He also designed two Java-like PDL orchestration workflows used by every infrastructure change path in the application, with pre/post execution checks and automated rollback guardrails as the org standard for change safety.

Eric leads distributed engineering teams across the US, Ireland, and India, with a focus on hiring, mentorship, and building cultures of autonomy, trust, and shared context.
